Andrew Mellor

Mathematician, Pythoneer, Cyclist


Quantatative Analyst. My research involves studying human online interactions, discerning patterns in behavior and assessing the importance of users to the system as a whole. Previously, I worked as part of the Oxford-Emirates Data Science Lab aiming to understand the complex relationships involved in the aviation industry. You can find my latest publications on my Google Scholar page.

I am a keen programmer, mainly programming in Python. I like to create scripts to do just about everything; data processing, model simulation, signing myself up to gym classes, and building websites (including this website). I am especially interested in visualisation tools and visualising networks and dynamics on them.

I love to cycle, whether it is hill climbing, racing, or touring across the country and continent. For those interested, you can follow me on Strava.
